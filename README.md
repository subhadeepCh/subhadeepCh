## 🚀 &nbsp; Revenue Control OS &nbsp;·&nbsp; <sub>the startup I'm building</sub>

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/STATUS-Building%20in%20public-6EE7B7?style=for-the-badge" />
  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/CATEGORY-RevOps%20%2F%20Sales%20Ops-3B82F6?style=for-the-badge" />

> ### _"Leads leak. SLAs die. Sales teams point fingers._
> ### _Revenue Control OS makes pipeline **accountable by default**."_

### 🧩 &nbsp; The problem

Inbound lead flow is chaos. WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, email, forms — leads pour in through 5+ channels with zero ownership. SDRs miss follow-ups. Managers have no visibility until the month closes. **Revenue quietly leaks out.**

### 💡 &nbsp; The product

<table>
  <tr>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <h4>📥 &nbsp; Omnichannel capture</h4>
      <sub>WhatsApp · Instagram · Email — one unified inbox, zero leads dropped.</sub>
    </td>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <h4>🎯 &nbsp; Auto-assignment + SLA</h4>
      <sub>Round-robin, territory, or custom routing. SLA timers that actually escalate.</sub>
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <h4>🔁 &nbsp; Automated follow-ups</h4>
      <sub>Sequences fire on inactivity. No manual bumping. No "I forgot."</sub>
    </td>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <h4>📊 &nbsp; Conversion analytics</h4>
      <sub>Funnel, drop-off, rep leaderboard — numbers that drive 1:1s, not decks.</sub>
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <h4>🚨 &nbsp; Escalation engine</h4>
      <sub>Stale leads escalate up the chain. Silent pipeline dies loud.</sub>
    </td>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <h4>🧠 &nbsp; Accountability layer</h4>
      <sub>Every lead has an owner, a timer, and a paper trail.</sub>
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>
